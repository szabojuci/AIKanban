# Good to know

## Good to have some extensions

As I am a **Linux** "user" and a **[VSCodium](https://vscodium.com/)** fan, I like to use free tools. **VSCodium** has a [new](https://vscodium-next.netlify.app/) website, but unfortunately currently is not up-to-date. These extensions are also available for:

- [Antigravity](https://antigravity.google/) (developed by Google)
- [Cursor](https://cursor.com/)
- [Kiro](https://kiro.dev/) (developed by Amazon)
- [WindSurf](https://windsurf.com/) (developed by Codeium)

but [VSCode](https://code.visualstudio.com/) has more because of its own marketplace.
